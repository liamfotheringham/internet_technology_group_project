What is Rango?


Rango is a social media link aggregation website!

But it is also so much more than that!

It's a place to find interesting articles shared by likeminded people.

It's also a place where you can connect with those same people and discuss almost any topic you can imagine!

Our Goals:


Our objectives in creating this website are to foster an information sharing community, encourage user engagement, and facilitate discussions on a wide variety of topics.

More specifically, our aims are to help introduce the wonderful world of programming and computer science to those who may not be familiar with it, but want to learn more.

We also want to help people discover new areas or topics within these subjects to deepen their knowledge.

All in all, we want to provide a place where education and socialising go hand in hand to super charge everybody's learning!
